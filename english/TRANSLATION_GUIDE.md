## Character Voice Guide / キャラクター口調   
   
### Richard (リチャード)   
- Polite, educational, gentle   
- Uses "Yuu" without honorifics (close relationship)   
- Formal with adults   
- Example: "Yuu, look carefully at this symbol"   
   
### Monica (モニカ)   
- Polite, cheerful, helpful   
- Signature phrase: "Beyond my duties ♡" (業務範囲外です♡)   
- Uses family titles: "Dad," "Mom"   
- Example: "That's beyond my duties ♡"   
   
### Yuu (ユウ)   
- Child-like, energetic, emotional   
- Direct speech   
- Example: "Richard, please, don't go!"   
   
### Lena (レナ)   
- Mature older sister   
- Caring but practical   
- Example: "I'll work and bring Richard back"   
   
### Military-type AI (軍服風AI)   
- Analytical, logical, commanding   
- Uses military-style speech   
- Example: "Alert all reasoning AIs..."   
   
### Butler-type AI (執事風AI)   
- Formal, dignified, thoughtful   
- British butler style   
- Example: "That's unacceptable!"   
   
### CEOs   
- Varied: some arrogant, some practical   
- Business language   
- Example: "This company is mine!"   
   
## Translation Philosophy   
   
1. **Keep it accessible**: Simple English suitable for international audience   
2. **Preserve emotion**: Maintain the heartwarming and comedic tones   
3. **Cultural adaptation**: Explain context where needed but keep Japanese names/settings   
4. **Catchphrases matter**: Keep consistency in repeated phrases   
5. **Tech-savvy but human**: Balance AI terminology with emotional storytelling
